What to include

Send enough context to make the first reply useful

  • A short description of the issue or request
  • The email address tied to the account, if relevant
  • Your device model and iOS version for app issues
  • The current BudgetGo app version
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if they clarify what happened

Avoid sending full payment details or sensitive financial information by email.
